Schnitzel

Home

This simple Schnitzel features crispy, golden-brown breaded cutlets made from pork. Coated in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, the schnitzels are fried to perfection, creating a satisfying crunch with each bite. Served with a lemon wedge for added freshness, it's a comforting and flavorful dish, perfect alongside potatoes or a crisp salad.

  • Pork loin or pork tenderloin
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)
  1. Season the pork cutlets with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge the pork in flour, then dip into beaten eggs, and coat with breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and fry the pork cutlets until golden and crispy on both sides.
  4. Remove from the pan and drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges for squeezing.
