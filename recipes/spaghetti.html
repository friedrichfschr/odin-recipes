This simple Spaghetti Bolognese features a rich, savory meat sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic, simmered to perfection. The sauce is lightly seasoned with oregano and pepper, creating a flavorful base that perfectly complements the tender pasta. Served with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, this dish is a comforting, hearty classic that's quick to prepare and sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Saut√© chopped onion, garlic, and carrot in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, and seasonings, then simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  5. Toss the pasta with the sauce and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
